Hi, I'm Jack.

I am an electrical engineer by training, and a software developer by craft.

My goal is to make it simple for scientists to play with their data. I accomplish this with intuitive UIs and powerful data processing. In my free time I like to explore and develop ideas in the world of human computer interfaces.

I recently led the winning team at the YCombinator Hardware Hackathon, where we designed and prototyped a novel form of human-computer interaction. I like Raspberry Pis and Python, so check out some of my projects involving both.

I am currently working on a book involving Raspberry Pis, home automation, mobile software, and possibly powerful lasers.
